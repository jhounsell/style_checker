Style guide checker for content designers

This tool checks if your content follows style guide rules. Copy and paste text into the text box and see if the content complies with different style guide rules.

Use the checker

What the checker will check

The checker will flag:

  • words or abbreviations to avoid
  • for correct usage of terms
  • where capitalisation is incorrect on specific terms
  • if a specific term should be 1 word instead of 2 (or vice versa)

What the checker won't check

The checker won't flag:

  • general capitalisation issues - only on specific terms
  • syntax or grammatical errors
  • incorrect American spellings
  • puncutation, spacing and formatting issues
  • readability or structural problems

Fundamentally, the tool must be used by a trained, skilled content author. It was designed to speed up checking and QA tasks. It can't tell you whether your content is 'good' and meets user needs.
